About Me

Over the course of three months, I was challenged like I never thought possible, and pushed to my absolute limits at a Web Developement Bootcamp. Although this program had its difficulties, I found myself immersed in the projects. Web Development opened my eyes to a new world of possibilities that I can't wait to explore creatively.

I have a huge desire for Design and Web Development. In everything I do, I put my 120%. I'm confident when I say that you probably haven't met someone quite like me. Coming from a Communications background, talking to people and being a team player has been an important role in everything I do. Getting along in the work place is super important to me.

When I start a project, the first thing I do is think about the client. Who is using this app? Who is the target audience? I immedietely start brainstorming about all the possible colors and sketches that fit the needs of the user while trying to make it super easy to use and fluid. You can count on me to have a lot of wireframes, moodboards, and mockups. This is really helpful when it comes to development because I have set measurements, colors, fontsizes, etc. all ready to go. For my group project at the bootcamp, I created an entire design system of styled-components and was able to make our website look almost identical to the mock-up.

What Now? I'm constantly learning and growing everyday. I know that everyday is an opportunity to be better and I want to take advantage of that. I know my weaknesses and my strengths and because of that I am confident that I can do pretty much anything I set my mind to. I'm excited for this next adventure and can't wait to learn, build, and create.
